about us

Your event requires careful preparation and creative execution.

Evanto is your partner in memory-making whether it is a corporate seminar, wedding or a private party. From the venue to décor to entertainment and everything in between (whatever it may be), we take care of every detail that goes into impeccable arrangements, customized according to your personal taste and needs.
